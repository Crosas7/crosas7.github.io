I’m Carlos Rosas (Creator of “Saoko Ink”), a tattoo artist driven by rhythm, storytelling, and intentional design. My work blends technical precision with an organic, intuitive flow — creating tattoos that sit naturally on the body and age with grace. I focus on bold silhouettes, balanced compositions, and meaning-packed details, whether it’s fine-line symbolism or heavier illustrative work.

Tattooing, for me, is more than an art form — it’s a conversation. Every session is a collaboration where creativity meets trust. I aim to create a calm, professional space where clients feel heard, understood, and excited about the art we build together. My goal is always to translate ideas into something timeless and personal, something that carries rhythm — “saoko” — in every line.

Beyond tattooing, I mentor incoming artists who want to learn about technique, design structure, branding, and digital marketing. I’ve built Saoko Ink not just as a portfolio, but as a resource hub for anyone looking to enter the tattoo world while balancing creativity with business. If you’d like to collaborate, ask questions, or book a session, feel free to reach out — Email me.

Aftercare & Education

  • Wash gently with fragrance-free soap 2–3× daily for the first week.
  • Moisturize lightly — avoid over-saturation.
  • No direct sun, pools, or heavy sweating during healing.
