About the Artist

I’m Carlos (“Saoko”), a tattoo artist focused on clean compositions, flow, and meaning. I mix technical precision with an organic rhythm — creating work that sits well on the body and ages cleanly.

Outside the chair, I share aftercare guidance and mentor newcomers in tattoos, business, and digital marketing.

Email me to collaborate.

Aftercare & Education

  • Wash gently with fragrance-free soap 2–3× daily for the first week.
  • Moisturize lightly — avoid over-saturation.
  • No direct sun, pools, or heavy sweating during healing.
